freeCodeCamp - Greater Sacramento Area

The freeCodeCamp group of the Greater Sacramento Area is great band of people who are working towards a common goal: to become better developers. They are open to anyone who is interested at any skill level and meet on a somewhat regular basis. During their Develop Hangout, the new people to the group make their introductions, and the rest of the meeting is spent going over members ongoing projects, along with feedback time. Their next meeting is an example of the other types of events they hold. where a member of the group volunteers to organize and lead a targeted discussion. For example, at the time of this writting, the next talk will be Using Git: From 0 to Production by Bryan Jenks.
